Tiropita

Servings: 2 | Amount Per Serving: half a pastry

Ingredients

  • 1) 3-4 sheets of phyllo pastry
  • 2) 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3) 1 block of feta
  • 4) 20-30g of honey
  • 5) 1-2 springs of thyme or rosemary/li>
  • 6) Sesame seeds

Steps

  • 1) Preheat the oven at 170ÂºC and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  • 2) Lay out the phyllo pastry sheets and brush about a tablespoon of olive oil.
  • 3) Place the block of feta in the middle and fold the phyllo pastry sheets like you're wrapping a parcel.
  • 4) Sprinkle with sesame seeds and bake for 20 minutes, turning halfway through.
  • 5) Once the pastry is done, top with honey and fresh thyme or rosemary.

Notes

  • 1) If phyllo is not available, any kind of pastry though will work.
