Blueberry Banana Bread

As Loaf: Servings: 16 | Amount Per Serving: 1 slice

  • Calories: 98 | Fat: 2g
  • Protein: 2g |Saturated Fat: 1g
  • Fiber: 2g | Sugar: 13g
  • Sodium: 87mg | Carbohydrates: 21g

As Muffins: Servings: 12 | Amount Per Serving: 1 muffin

  • Prep Time: 15mins
  • Cook Time: 50mins
  • Total Time: 1hr 5mins

Ingredients

  • 1) 1 cup blueberries
  • 2) 3 ripe medium bananas, mashed
  • 3) 2 tbsp unsweetened apple sauce (or substitute for olive oil)
  • 4) 1.25 cups white whole wheat flour
  • 5) 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 6) 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 7) 2 tablespoon butter, softened
  • 8) 1/2 cup (4 oz) light brown sugar, packed
  • 9) 2 large egg whites
  • 10) 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  • 1) 1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Lightly spray a 8 x 4 loaf pan with baking spray.
  • 2) In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt with a wire whisk. Set aside.
  • 3) In a large bowl cream butter and sugar with an electric mixer.
  • 4) Add egg whites, bananas, apple sauce and vanilla, and beat at medium speed until thick. Scrape down sides of the bowl.
  • 5) Add flour mixture and blend at low speed until combined, do not over mix.
  • 6) Fold in the blueberries and pour batter into the prepared loaf pans.
  • 7) Bake in the center rack for about 50 to 55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • 8) Let the pan cool at least 20 minutes, bread should be room temperature before slicing.

Notes

  • 1) Muffins need slightly less cooking time.
  • 2) Add nuts or cinnamon for added flavor.
  • 3) Can use whole egg instead of only whites.
