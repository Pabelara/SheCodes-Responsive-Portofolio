Blueberry Oatmeal Peach

Serving Size: 1 | Amount Per Serving: 1

  • Calories: 377 | Total Fat: 14g
  • Carbohydrates: 57g | Dietary Fiber: 10g
  • Sugar: 22g | Protein: 11g

Ingredients

  • 1) 1/2 cup old fashioned oatmeal
  • 2) 1 cup water
  • 3) 1 cup fresh peaches, chopped into small chunks and divided
  • 4) 1/2 cup fresh blueberries, divided
  • 5) 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6) 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 7) 1/2 Tablespoon ground flaxseed
  • 8) Tablespoon almond butter (or other nut butter)

Steps

  • 1) Add oatmeal, water, ½ cup peaches, ¼ cup blueberries, vanilla and cinnamon into a small saucepan.
  • 2) Cook over medium heat for 5-6 minutes or until oatmeal has thickened and is the consistency you like.
  • 3) Spoon into a serving bowl. Top with remaining peaches, blueberries, ground flaxseed and nut butter.
