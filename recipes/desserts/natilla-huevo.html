Natilla de Huevo

Servings: 3 small bowls | Amount Per Serving: 1 bowl

  • Prep Time: 5mins
  • Cook Time: 15mins
  • Total Time: 20mins

Ingredients

  • 1) 1 can evaporated milk (450g)
  • 2) 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 3) Sliver of lemon peel
  • 4) 1 cinnamon stick
  • 5) Vanilla extract
  • 6) 2 egg yolks
  • 7) 2 teaspoons of corn starch

Steps

  • 1) Add milk, sugar, lemon peel, cinnamon stick and a drizzle of vanilla into a pot.
  • 2) Heat slowly until mix boils while gently stirring.
  • 3) Allow mix to cool.
  • 4) Separate egg yolks from whites (filter yolks for smoother texture) and beat.
  • 5) Add egg yolks to mix and stir.
  • 6) Return pot to fire at mid-heat and allow to simmer.
  • 7) Mix corn starch with an inch of water and stir before pouring into mix.
  • 8) Stir mix until it becomes a thick cream.
  • 9) Stir mix until it becomes a thick cream.
  • 10) Add sprinkle of cinnamon on top.
  • 11) Place cake into oven at medium height for 30 minutes.

Notes

  • 1) The more corn flour you add to the mix, the thicker it becomes.
