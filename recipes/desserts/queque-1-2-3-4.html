Queque 1-2-3-4

Servings: 8 slices | Amount Per Serving: 1 slice

  • Prep Time: 30mins
  • Cook Time: 30mins
  • Total Time: 1hr

Ingredients

  • 1) 4 eggs
  • 2) 1 vanilla yogurt
  • 3) 1 cup of olive oil
  • 4) 2 cups of sugar
  • 5) 1.5 cups of flour
  • 6) 1.5 cups of corn flour
  • 7) 15mg of baking powder
  • 8) Ingredients of flavor of choice (orange or lemond rind, chocolate chips, extracts, etc.)

Steps

  • 1) Pre-heat oven at 180ºC.
  • 2) Separate yolks from whites and whip whites until fluffy.
  • 3) Add sugar slowly while continuing to whip mix.
  • 4) Add flour slowly while continuing to whip mix.
  • 5) Add corn flour slowly while continuing to whip mix.
  • 6) Add baking powder.
  • 7) Add yolks one by one while continuing to whip mix.
  • 8) Add olive oil slowly.
  • 9) Add yogurt and a drizzle of vanilla extract.
  • 10) Spread mold with butter and sprinkle with flour or pour drizzle of olive oil to avoid cake sticking to mold.
  • 11) Place cake into oven at medium height for 30 minutes.
  • 12) After the 30 minutes have gone by test cake readiness by pricking the cake with toothpick.
    If toothpick is dry then cake is ready, otherwise give it a few more minutes in the oven.

Notes

  • 1) Cup measurement is determined by size of yogurt, standard measure is 125g.
