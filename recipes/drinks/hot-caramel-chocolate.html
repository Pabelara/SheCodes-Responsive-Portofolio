Hot Caramel Chocolate

Servings: 6 | Amount Per Serving: 1/6

  • Calories: 213 | Total Fat: 7g
  • Saturated Fat: 4g | Cholesterol: 19mg
  • Carbohydrates: 29g | Protein: 10g
  • Sodium: 133mg
  • Prep Time: 5mins
  • Cook Time: 10mins
  • Total Time: 15mins

Ingredients

  • 1) 1/3 cups of sugar
  • 2) 1/3 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3) 1/3 cups of water
  • 4) 6 milk-chocolate-covered round caramels
  • 5) 6 cups of milk, half-and-half or light cream
  • 6) Whipped cream optional
  • 7) Milk chocolate-covered and/or candy-coated round caramels (optional)

Steps

  • 1) In a large saucepan combine sugar, cocoa powder, and water. Cook and stir over medium heat until sugar is dissolved.
  • 2) Add candies; cook and stir until melted. Stir in milk. Heat through.
  • 3) Pour into mugs; top with whipped cream and additional caramel candies. Makes 6 servings.
