Baked Tomato, Mozzarella & Basil Risotto

Servings: 4 | Amount Per Serving: 1/4th

  • Calories: 504 | Total Fat: 16g
  • Carbohydrates: 71g | Saturated Fat: 7g
  • Sugar:5g | Protein: 16g
  • Sodium: 1110mg | Dietary Fiber: 4g
  • Prep Time: 10mins
  • Cook Time: 30mins
  • Total Time: 40mins

Ingredients

  • 1) 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2) 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3) 300g arborio rice
  • 4) Small glass of white wine (optional), or 100ml extra stock
  • 5) 750ml hot vegetable stock
  • 6) 100g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 7) Small handful of basil leaves, finely shredded, plus a few extra small leaves to serve
  • 8) 25g parmesan or vegetarian alternative, finely grated
  • 9) 150g ball mozzarella, torn into small chunks
  • 10) 25g dried breadcrumbs

Steps

  • 1) Heat the oven to 200C/180C fan/gas
  • 2) Heat half the oil in an ovenproof pan or casserole dish, then stir in the onion and cook over a low heat for 8-10 mins until golden.
  • 3) Scatter over the rice and mix well until coated.
    Pour over the wine, if using, and cook for 2 mins until absorbed.
    Pour over the hot stock, then give the rice a quick stir.
  • 4) Put the dish in the oven and bake the rice for 10 mins.
  • 5) Remove from the oven and stir through the tomatoes, basil, half of the parmesan and some seasoning.
    Flatten everything out roughly with the back of a spoon, then nestle the chunks of mozzarella evenly into the risotto.
  • 6) Sprinkle over the breadcrumbs and the remaining parmesan, and drizzle with the rest of the olive oil.
  • 7) Put the dish back in the oven, and bake everything for another 10-15 mins until the rice is just cooked through, heating it under the grill if you want a deep, golden crust.
  • 8) Leave to stand for a few minutes, scatter with a few extra basil leaves and serve scooped straight from the dish.

Notes

  • 1) Comments suggest adding more tomatoes and onions to make flavor more intense.
  • 2) Consider adding italian seasoning
