Caprese Pasta

Servings: 4 | Amount Per Serving: 1/4

  • Calories: 529 | Total Fat: 28g
  • Carbohydrates: 53g | Saturated Fat: 7g
  • Cholesterol: 22mg| Protein: 15g
  • Sodium: 481mg | Sugar: 8g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Prep Time: 20mins
  • Cook Time: 20min
  • Total Time: 40min

Ingredients

  • 1) 8 ounces angel hair pasta
  • 2) 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3) 0.25 cup red onion
  • 4) 0.25 cup red bell pepper
  • 5) 3 cloves garlic
  • 6) 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 7) 1 teaspoon lemon juice, fresh-squeezed
  • 8) 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 9) 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 10) 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
  • 11) 0.25 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 12) 0.25 cup basil
  • 13) 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese

Steps

  • 1) Bring a large pot of water to boil on the stove.
  • 2) On another burner on the stove, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat, add the red onion and bell pepper.
  • 3) Sauté for 2-3 minutes, then add the garlic and sauté for an additional minute.
  • 4) Add the cherry tomatoes to the skillet with the Italian seasoning, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  • 5) Continue cooking, stirring occasionally until the tomatoes begin to burst, 6-8 minutes.
  • 6) While the tomatoes are cooking, add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package directions.
  • 7) Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet with the tomatoes.
  • 8) Add the balsamic vinegar and remaining 4 tablespoons olive oil to the skillet.
  • 9) Toss to combine everything.
  • 10) Remove from the heat and stir in the fresh basil and mozzarella cheese.
  • 11) Serve immediately.
