Honey-Lime Meatballs

Servings: 15 | Amount per Serving: 4 meatballs

  • Calories: 136 | Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fiber: 1g | Protein: 14g
  • Sodium: 265mg | Cholesterol: 61mg
  • Total Fat: 4g | Sugar: 5g
  • Prep Time: 30mins
  • Cook Time: 4hrs
  • Total Time: 4hrs 30mins

Ingredients

  • 1) Cooking spray
  • 2) 680 gr 95% ground beef
  • 3) 200 gr uncooked hot Italian chicken or turkey sausage links, casings removed
  • 4) 1 cup quick-cooking rolled oats
  • 5) 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 6) 2 large egg whites
  • 7) 1 cup finely chopped green onions
  • 8) 4 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 9) 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 10) Salt

Sauce

  • 11) 0.25 cup honey
  • 12) 0.25 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 13)2 tablespoons lime juice

Steps

  • 1) Coat a 3 1/2- or 4-quart slow cooker with cooking spray; set aside.
  • 2) In a large bowl combine ground beef, sausage, oats, egg product, green onions, Worcestershire sauce, and crushed red pepper.
  • 3) Shape mixture into 72 approximately 2-inch meatballs.
  • 4) Coat a 12-inch nonstick skillet with cooking spray. Cook meatballs, one-fourth at a time, over medium heat until brown, turning once.
    Transfer to prepared cooker.
  • 5) Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 4 to 5 hours or on high-heat setting for 2 to 2 1/2 hours.
  • 6) In a small bowl whisk together honey, soy sauce, and lime juice. Pour over meatballs. Toss gently to coat.
  • 7) Serve immediately or keep warm, covered, on warm or low-heat setting for up to 1 hour.
