Kofta

Servings: 3-4

  • Prep Time: 15mins
  • Cook Time: 15mins
  • Total Time: 30mins

Ingredients

  • 1) 14 wooden skewers
  • 2) 2 tablespoons finely minced garlic
  • 3) 0.25 cup red onion shredded and squeezed of all liquid
  • 4) 0.25 cup fresh parsley minced
  • 5) 0.25 cup fresh mint minced
  • 6) 1 tablespoon fresh ginger grated
  • 7) 0.5 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8) 2 tablespoons Garam Masala
  • 9) 1 pound ground lamb
  • 10) Greek flat bread
  • 11) Shredded lettuce
  • 12) Diced tomato
  • 13 Tzatziki sauce

Steps

  • 1) Soak skewers in water for two hours or longer.
  • 2) If using an outdoor grill, heat to medium heat. Or use a ribbed grill pan on your stove.
  • 3) In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients except lamb until combined. Then add lamb and mix just enough to combine without over working the meat.
  • 4) Form into seven oval shaped patties.
  • 5) Place two skewers side by side into each. (Two makes it easy to flip on grill).
  • 6) Gill over medium heat flipping halfway through to a nicely seared exterior and cooked to your liking on the inside.
    We cooked ours to medium which took about 12-15 minutes.
  • 7) To serve, place one or two patties on open flat bread and top with shredded lettuce, chopped tomato and a generous drizzle of Tzatziki .

Notes

  • 1) Add garlic.
  • 2) Spice mixture might be too intense, consider halving garam masala and mint.
