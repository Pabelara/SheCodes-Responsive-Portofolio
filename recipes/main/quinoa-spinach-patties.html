Quinoa and Spinach Patties

Servings: 7 | Amount Per Serving: 2 patties

  • Calories: 236 | Total Fat: 7.5g
  • Carbohydrates: 30.5g | Saturated Fat: 2g
  • Sugar: 2g | Protein: 11.5g
  • Sodium: 429.5mg | Cholesterol: 110mg
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Prep Time: 5mins
  • Cook Time: 55mins
  • Total Time: 1hr

Ingredients

  • 1) 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 2) 2 cups water
  • 3) 4 eggs, whisked
  • 4) 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 5) 3 large scallions, sliced thin
  • 6) 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 7) 0.5 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8) 1 cup steamed spinach, chopped (frozen is fine)
  • 9) 1 cup plain breadcrumbs
  • 10) 1 teaspoon olive oil

Steps

  • 1) Rinse the quinoa thoroughly and place the grains in a medium sauce pan with 2 cups of water. Bring the water to a boil and reduce to a simmer.
  • 2) Cook until quinoa is tender and has absorbed the liquid, about 20 minutes. Let it cool.
  • 3) In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, eggs, Parmesan, scallions, garlic, salt, steamed spinach, and breadcrumbs.
    Let everything sit for a few minutes to absorb the liquid. The batter should be moist, but not runny. Form patties 0.25 cup each.
  • 4) Heat the oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-low heat. In 2 or 3 batches, cook the patties covered for 8-10 minutes on each side, or until browned and golden.

Notes

  • 1) Add other vegetables such as onions or peppers.
  • 2) Add dried oregano, basil and/or thyme to the breacrumbs for added flavor.
  • 3) Serve with lemon or a lemon-yogurt dip.
  • 4) Substitute breadcrumbs for almond flour or oats. In this case, add extra herbs.
