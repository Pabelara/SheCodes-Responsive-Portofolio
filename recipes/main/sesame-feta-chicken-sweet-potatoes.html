Sesame Feta Chicken
and Sweet Potatoes

Servings: 6 | Amount per Serving: 1/6

  • Calories: 563
  • Prep Time: 20mins
  • Cook Time: 30mins
  • Total Time: 50mins

Ingredients

For the Chicken and Potatoes:

  • 1) 2 sweet potatoes (2-3lbs), cut into match sticks
  • 2) 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3) Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4) 6 boneless skinless chicken breasts or thighs (1.5lbs)
  • 5) 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 6) 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 7) 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 8) 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 9) 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 10) 4 cups shredded kale
  • 11) 1 cup mixed herbs: basil, dill, parsley
  • 12) 1 avocado, sliced or chopped
  • 13) 0.75 cup crumbled feta cheese

For the Sauce:

  • 1) 0.33 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2) 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 3) 1 tablespoon stone ground mustard
  • 4) 3 tablespoons honey
  • 5) 2 tablespoons tahini or mayo
  • 6) 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Steps

  • 1) Preheat oven to 220Â°C.
  • 2) On a baking sheet, toss the sweet potatoes with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, salt and black pepper.
  • 3) Bake for 10 minutes.
  • 4) In a bowl, toss together 2 tablespoons of olive oil, the chicken, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  • 5) Nestle the chicken around the potatoes.
    Sprinkle the sesame seeds over each piece of chicken
  • 6) Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through.
  • 7) To make the dressing, combine all ingredients and whisk until smooth.
  • 8) In a bowl, combine the kale and herbs. Drizzle a few tablespoons of dressing over the kale and massage into the greens.
  • 9) Remove the chicken from the oven. Add the greens, the avocado, and crumbled feta.
  • 10) Serve with additional dressing.

Notes

  • 1) Substitute kale for spinach.
  • 2) Substitute feta for goat cheese.
  • 3) Consider adding the greens to the oven for the last 5-10 minutes..
  • 4) Add toasted almonds or pecans for an additional crunch.
  • 5) Herbs quantity might be too much, test and reduce.
  • 6) The tahini and mayo are used to add creaminess to the dresssing.
    They have different flavor profiles, but both are tasty.
