Slow Cooker Italian Stuffed Peppers

Servings: 3 | Amount Per Serving: 1 stuffed pepper

  • Calories: 265 | Total Fat: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 15g | Saturated Fat: 4g
  • Cholesterol: 61mg| Protein: 28g
  • Sodium: 938mg | Sugar: 9g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Prep Time: 30mins
  • Cook Time: 6hrs
  • Total Time: 6hrs 30min

Ingredients

  • 1) 0.5 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2) 0.25 cup onion, chopped
  • 3) 0.5 cup baby bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 4) 0.5 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 5) 0.5 lb ground turkey
  • 6) 0.5 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 7) 0.75 cups chicken broth, dividedd
  • 8) 3 bell peppers
  • 9) 0.5 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 10) 1 cups marinara sauce
  • 11) 0.25 cup parmesan cheese, optional topping

Steps

  • 1) Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • 2) Add the onions & mushrooms, saute 3-4 minutes, then add the garlic and saute for an additional minute.
  • 3) Add the ground turkey and cook through, drain any grease once cooked through.
  • 4) Add the Italian seasoning.
  • 5) Pour 0.5 cup chicken broth into the skillet.
  • 6) Stir everything together, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • 7) Remove from the heat and set aside to cool slightly.
  • 8) Pour the remaining cup of chicken broth in the bottom of a large slow cooker.
  • 9) Remove the stems and seeds from the bell peppers and discard.
  • 10) Divide the turkey and mushroom mixture into each bell pepper.
  • 11) Fill with marinara sauce.
  • 12) Top with a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  • 13) Place each filled pepper in the crock pot.
    Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 2-3 hours.
  • 14) Remove from the slow cooker and serve with parmesan cheese on top.
