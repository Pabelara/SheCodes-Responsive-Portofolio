Thai Style Chicken Noodles

Servings: 4 | Amount per Serving: 1/4

  • Prep Time: 10mins
  • Cook Time: 30min
  • Total Time: 40min

Ingredients

  • 1) 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2) 500g chicken, minced
  • 3) 3 spring onions, sliced
  • 4) 2 tablespoons red Thai curry paste
  • 5) 100g sugar snap peas or mange tout
  • 6) 200ml coconut milk
  • 7) 150ml chicken stock
  • 8) 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 9) 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 10) 3-4 egg noodle nests
  • 11) Sesame oil

For the Red Thai Curry Paste:

  • 1) 5cm ginger, peeled
  • 2) 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 3) 1 shallot, peeled
  • 4) 1 blade lemon grass, roughly chopped
  • 5) 15g fresh coriander/cilantro
  • 6) 2 tablespoons tomato purée
  • 7) 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 8) 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 9) 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 10) 2 teaspoons honey
  • 11) 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 12) Juice of 1 lime

Steps

For the Red Thai Curry Paste:

  • 1) Add all the ingredients to a blender and blitz until smooth.

For the Pad Thai:

  • 1) Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • 2) Add the garlic and cook until the garlic is golden, 2-3 minutes.
  • 3) Add the spring onions and peas and cook for 5 minutes.
  • 4) In the meantime, prepare the noodles in a separate pot.
  • 5) Add in the chicken and cook for another five minutes.
  • 6) Add the Thai paste, soy and tahini, stirring to mix well.
  • 7) Add the chicken stock followed by the coconut milk to the chicken pan. Simmer for a minute then add the drained noodles.
    Stir so the noodles are combined with the chicken & it’s sauce.
  • 8) Simmer for 2 minutes then serve.

Notes

  • 1) Consider adding different kinds of vegetables such as bell pepper, mushroom, cabbage or carrot.
    Make sure to sautée these vegetables alongside the leeks at the beginning of the cooking process.
  • 2) The lemongrass, coriander, lime juice, and tomato purée can be safely omitted from the paste.
  • 3) Substitute shallots and spring onions for leeks.
  • 4) Substitute sugar snap peas for regular peas.
  • 5) Substitute honey for brown sugar.
  • 6) Substitute tahini for peanut butter, keep in mind the flavor profile will be remarkably different.
  • 7) Substitute the egg noodles for any kind of asian style noodles.
  • 8) Sautée sliced almonds alongside the chicken for an additional crunch.
