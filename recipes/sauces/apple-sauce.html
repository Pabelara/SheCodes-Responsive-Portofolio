Apple Sauce

    Servings: 12 servings (3 cups) | Amount Per Serving: 1/4 cup

  • Calories: 55 | Total Fat: 8.3g
  • Sodium: 1mg | Sugar: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 15g | Dietary Fiber: 2g
  • Prep Time: 15min
  • Cook Time: 6hrs
  • Total Time: 6hrs 15min

Ingredients

  • 1) 8 medium apples, use a combination of Golden Delicious, Honey Crisp, Fuji, Gala, etc.
  • 2) 1 strips of lemon peel
  • 3) 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 4) 3 inch cinnamon stick or 0.5 teaspoons ground cinnnamon
  • 5) 5 teaspoons light brown sugar, unpacked

Steps

  • 1) Peel, core, and slice the apples.
  • 2) Place them in the slow cooker.
  • 3) Add the cinnamon stick, lemon peel, lemon juice and brown sugar.
  • 4) Set crock pot to low and cook for 6 hours.
  • 5) Stir apples occasionally, apples will slowly become a delicious applesauce.
  • 6) Remove cinnamon stick and use an immersion blender to blend until smooth or if you prefer a chunky sauce, leave sauce intact.

Notes

  • 1) Sugar can be left out for a sugar free recipe.
  • 2) Homemade applesauce can be refrigerated for a week to ten days;
    it can also be frozen or canned for longer-term storage.
