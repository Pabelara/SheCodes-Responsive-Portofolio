Dark Chocolate Fudge Sauce

    Servings: 3 cups

  • Total Time: 20min

Ingredients

  • 1) 0.5 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2) 0.25 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3) 142gr bittersweet chocolate, coarsely chopped
  • 4) 0.25 cup butter
  • 5) 0.25 cup buttermilk or 0.25 cup milk and 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 6) 1 cup whipping cream
  • 7) 0.5 cup light-color corn syrup
  • 8) 1 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  • 1) In a medium heavy saucepan combine brown sugar and cocoa powder. Stir in bittersweet chocolate, butter, and buttermilk.
  • 2) Cook and stir over medium heat until chocolate and butter are melted and mixture is smooth.
  • 3) Stir in cream and corn syrup. Cook and stir for 4 to 6 minutes or just until mixture is bubbly around edge.
  • 4) Remove from heat; stir in vanilla. Serve warm.

Notes

  • 1) Corn syrup can be substituted for 1 cup sugar dissolved in 0.25 cup warm water or 1 cup of honey.
  • 2) Transfer any leftover sauce to an airtight container; cover.
    Store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat before serving.
