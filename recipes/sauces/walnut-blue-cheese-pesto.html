Walnut and Blue Cheese Pesto

    Servings: 1 cup

  • Calories: 980 | Total Fat: 101g
  • Saturated Fat: 11g | Protein: 13g
  • Carbohydrates: 11g | Cholesterol: 25mg
  • Sugar: 2g | Sodium: 489mg
  • Total Time: 10min

Ingredients

  • 1) 2 cup basil
  • 2) 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3) 0.25 cup walnuts
  • 4) 0.25 cup blue cheese, crumbled
  • 5) 0.25 cup green onions, chopped
  • 6) 0.5 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 7) 1/3 cup olive oil

Steps

  • 1) In a food processor pulse all ingredients except olive oil until smooth.
  • 2) Slowly add the olive oil while pulsing.

Notes

  • 1) Store in a sealed container and refrigerate until ready to use or you can freeze this in ziplocks bags to use at a later date.
  • 2) Add evaporated milk to soften taste and reduce use of olive oil.
