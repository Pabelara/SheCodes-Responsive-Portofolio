Fried Cabbage

Servings: 6 | Amount Per Serving: 1/2 cup

  • Calories: 142 | Total Fat: 9g
  • Saturated Fat: 3g | Carbohydrates: 12g
  • Cholesterol 15mg | Protein: 5g
  • Sodium: 229mg | Fiber: 4g
  • Sugar: 6g | Vitamin A: 202IU
  • Vitamin C: 57mg | Calcium: 72mg
  • Potassium: 343mg | Iron: 1mg
  • Prep Time: 10mins
  • Cook Time: 20mins
  • Total Time: 30mins

Ingredients

  • 1) 6 pieces bacon, cut into 2.5cm pieces
  • 2) 1 small onion, diced
  • 3) 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4) 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 5) 0.25 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 6) 1 head cabbage, sliced and chopped
  • 7) 2 carrots, shredded
  • 8) Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 9) Dried oregano, to taste
  • 10) Onion and garlic powder, to taste

Steps

  • 1) Heat a large pan on medium heat and add the sliced bacon. Cook until crispy, then remove to a paper towel-lined plate.
    Remove some bacon grease, leaving about 2 tablespoons in the pan.
  • 2) Add the onion and saute for 2 to 3 minutes, or until it's translucent.
  • 3) Add the garlic, mustard, smoked paprika, salt and pepper and give it a stir to combine.
  • 4) Place the sliced cabbage and carrot on top and use a large spoon or tongs to stir and saut√©e together for another 12 to 15 minutes.
    When the cabbage is done it should be buttery soft and slightly caramelized.
  • 5) Toss the bacon back in, and stir it all together before serving.

Notes

  • 1) Consider adding a drizzle of honey to offset the tartness of the mustard.
