Savory Smashed Sweet Potatoes

Servings: 6 | Amount Per Serving: 0.5 potato

  • Calories: 206 | Total Fat: 8.5g
  • Carbohydrates: 29g | Saturated Fat: 3.5g
  • Cholesterol 14mg | Protein: 4g
  • Sodium: 310mg | Fiber: 4.5g
  • Sugar: 9g
  • Prep Time: 15mins
  • Cook Time: 35mins
  • Total Time: 50mins

Ingredients

  • 1) Olive oil spray
  • 2) 3 long, thin sweet potatoes (10 ounces each)
  • 3) 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 4) 0.75 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 5) Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 6) 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 7) 0.5 teaspoon onion powder
  • 8) 2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme, plus more for garnish
  • 9) 0.25 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 10) Nonfat Greek yogurt for serving, optional

Steps

  • 1) Spray a sheet pan with olive oil. Preheat oven to 425ºF | 218ºC.
  • 2) Wash and dry sweet potatoes then cut each into 0.75-inch thick round slices.
  • 3) In a large bowl, combine melted butter with salt, pepper, garlic powder and thyme. Add sweet potatoes and toss to evenly coat.
  • 4) Spread sweet potatoes in an even layer on the prepared sheet pan. Bake for 25 minutes, or until fork tender. Remove and allow to cool for 5 minutes.
  • 5) Adjust oven rack to the second from the top and switch oven to high broil.
  • 6) Using a fork, gently smash each slice.
  • 7) Spray lightly with olive oil then top each with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and return to upper middle rack of the oven.
  • 8) Broil for 3-5 minutes, until cheese is melted, and edges are crispy.
  • 9) Garnish with thyme and a small dollop of yogurt, if using.
